By now, I'm sure everyone knows about or has played Minecraft on at least one platform. If you aren't familiar with Minecraft, the entire game is an open world for you to build anything you can possibly think of, you need to gather your resources, craft the items you need, and put it all together to create your masterpiece. I personally have put countless hours into this game, and it is my go to game when I need to take my mind off of something. You get so into the game, that everything on your mind goes away, and it's even more effective if you play with a group of people.
Minecraft is one of the very few games that I have played that I can not criticize. There are endless amounts of possibilities of what you can build. Your ideas determine how much you enjoy the game, this game is the perfect time-killer for anyone that needs time to vent.


Rate: 5, uplifting
